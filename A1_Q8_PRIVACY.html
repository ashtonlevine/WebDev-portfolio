Privacy Policy

Here at Paws and Hearts Pet Adoption, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Our goal is to ensure the security of your personal information.

Information Collection and Use

We collect only the necessary information required to aid in the facilitation of pet adoptions and ensure the well-beings of our loving animals. this includes:

  • Name and contact information.
  • Email for direct communication.

Data Protection Commitment

We promise that your personal information will:

  • Never be sold to third parties.
  • Never be shared with unknown/unauthorized individuals.
  • only be used for pet adoption purposes.
  • Be stored in a secure manner.

Disclaimer

Please note:

  • Pet information is provided by current owners and cannot be independently verified by Paws and Hearts Pet Adoption.
  • We are not responsible for any inaccuracies in pet listings or descriptions.
  • Adoptions should be concluded after personally meeting and evaluating the pet.
  • We are not liable for any issues that could potentially arise after adoption.
  • Health and behavioural analysis of a pet before or after adoption should be conducted by a medical professional.

User Responsibilities

by using our website, you acknowledge that:

  • You will provide accurate information about yourself and any pets you list/ed.
  • You will use the website in a responsible manner and follow our terms of service.
  • you understand that pet adoption is a serious commitment and requires careful consideration.
